About Us

Serving Utah County, Salt Lake County & Summit County

Anderson Family Tree Experts is a locally owned and operated tree care company based in Orem, Utah. Led by Daniel Anderson, we bring skill, professionalism, and a genuine passion for trees to every job — big or small.

From removing towering pines to shaping ornamental bushes, we treat every property with the same care and respect we'd show our own. Daniel takes the time to walk you through the work, explain every cut, and make sure you're fully satisfied before he leaves.

Whether you're a homeowner protecting your roof or a contractor managing a large lot, you'll get the same responsive service, honest pricing, and quality results that have made us the trusted name in Utah County, Salt Lake County, and Summit County tree care.

— Daniel Anderson, Owner
